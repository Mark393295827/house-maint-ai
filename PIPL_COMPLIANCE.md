## 2. Data Minimization & Collection
- **WeChat OpenID over Phone Numbers:** Where possible, we rely on WeChat OpenID for session management rather than storing plain-text phone numbers.
- **Location Fuzzing:** Exact GPS coordinates are required for worker matching but are fuzzed to a 500-meter radius when stored in historical analytics.
- **Photo Retention:** Maintenance photos are auto-deleted 1 year after ticket resolution. Users can request early deletion.

---

## 3. PIPL Data Residency (The Legal Moat)
- **Mainland Cloud:** All user data, including SQLite/PostgreSQL databases and image blobs, must reside on servers physically located within mainland China (e.g., Aliyun, Tencent Cloud).
- **No Cross-border Transfer:** No user-generated content (UGC) is sent to APIs hosted outside of China without explicit, separate user consent.
- **AI Model Providers:** Only mainland-hosted LLMs (DeepSeek, Baidu ERNIE) are used for production inference. No data leaves PRC borders.

---

## 4. AI Processing & Privacy (Face/Data Blurring)
Because users upload photos of leaks/damage that may inadvertently capture sensitive personal items or faces:
- **Pre-processing:** All images submitted to the AI for diagnosis undergo a local, edge-based (or mainland-server-based) anonymization pass.
- **Face Blurring:** OpenCV or equivalent lightweight model detects and blurs human faces *before* the image payload is sent to the multimodal LLM (DeepSeek/Baidu ERNIE).
- **Guardrail:** If face blurring fails or detects no faces in a photo with people, the image is quarantined for human review.

---

## 5. Consent Lifecycle
- **Mini-program Authorization:** Explicit consent is requested during the WeChat Mini Program onboarding flow specifically for "Processing of maintenance photos for AI analysis."
- **Granular Consent:** Users can separately consent to: (1) photo processing, (2) location access, (3) voice recording.
- **Right to Erasure:** A dedicated `DELETE /api/v1/users/me` endpoint ensures all associated records (orders, reports, tokens) are cascadingly erased per PIPL requirements.
- **Consent Withdrawal:** Users can withdraw consent at any time via Mini Program settings. Withdrawal stops new data collection but does not retroactively delete legally required records (e.g., tax records).

---

## 6. User-Facing Privacy Disclosures

### Required Disclosures (per PIPL Art. 17)
The following disclosures are presented to users during onboarding:

1. **"We process your maintenance photos using AI."** — Photos are analyzed by AI to diagnose issues. Faces are automatically blurred before processing.
2. **"We use your location to find nearby workers."** — Your GPS coordinates are used once for matching and then fuzzed to a 500m radius for storage.
3. **"Your data stays in China."** — All personal data is stored on servers in mainland China. No data is transferred abroad.
4. **"You can delete your data anytime."** — Account deletion removes all personal data, photos, and conversation history. Tax records are retained as required by law.

## 8. Privacy Incident Response

| Severity | Example | Response Time | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Unblurred faces sent to LLM | Immediate | System halt, PIPL officer notified, affected users contacted within 72 hours |
| **High** | Data exfiltration attempt detected | < 1 hour | IP blocked, audit log review, authorities notified if confirmed |
| **Medium** | Excess data retained beyond policy | < 24 hours | Automated purge triggered, process review |
| **Low** | Consent UI unclear to users | < 1 week | UX update deployed |
