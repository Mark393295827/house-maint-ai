## Persona 1: "小美" (Xiǎo Měi) — The Frazzled Tenant

### Demographics
| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 28 |
| Location | Sanya, Hainan — rents a 2BR apartment in a mid-rise complex |
| Tech Comfort | High — uses WeChat for everything (payments, messaging, work) |
| Language | Mandarin with Hainan dialect at home |
| Income | ¥8,000/month |

### Daily Workflow
- Works 9-6 at a tourism management company. Communicates with clients via WeChat all day.
- Returns home to a rental apartment. Has never met the landlord (absentee owner from Beijing).
- When something breaks, she messages the property manager on WeChat and waits 1-3 days for a response.

### Core Frustrations
| Pain Point | Severity (1-5) |
|---|---|
| Can't describe the problem technically ("water is coming from somewhere under the sink") | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Feels unsafe letting unknown workers into her apartment | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Property manager takes 2+ days to arrange a fix | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Disputes over who pays — landlord damage vs. tenant damage | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Downloading a new app is unacceptable | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

### Goals
1. Get an instant answer: "Is this an emergency or can it wait?"
2. Resolve simple issues myself without involving anyone.
3. If a worker must come, know who they are and when they'll arrive.

### JTBD Statement
> **When** I discover something broken in my rental apartment, **I want to** send a quick photo or voice note and get an immediate AI assessment, **so I can** know whether it's urgent, whether I can fix it myself, and avoid days of waiting for the property manager to respond.

## Persona 2: "张总" (Zhāng Zǒng) — The Overloaded Property Manager

### Demographics
| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 42 |
| Location | Sanya — manages 300 apartments across 5 buildings |
| Tech Comfort | Moderate — uses WeChat, spreadsheets, and basic SaaS tools |
| Language | Mandarin |
| Role | "二房东" (sublease landlord) — leases entire floors from owners |

### Daily Workflow
- Wakes up to 15-30 WeChat messages from tenants overnight (complaints, maintenance requests, lease questions).
- Manually triages: calls a worker for urgent issues, promises to "look into it" for non-urgent ones.
- Tracks maintenance costs in a WeChat group chat + Excel spreadsheet.
- Reports monthly to building owners on maintenance spend and tenant satisfaction.

### Core Frustrations
| Pain Point | Severity (1-5) |
|---|---|
| Drowning in unstructured WeChat messages from tenants | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Can't determine fault (landlord vs. tenant damage) from photos | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| No centralized record of maintenance history per unit | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Workers quote inconsistently — no price transparency | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Manual coordination wastes 3+ hours/day | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

### Goals
1. Auto-triage incoming complaints by severity and responsibility.
2. Deflect simple issues with DIY guides to reduce worker dispatches.
3. Have a clean dashboard for monthly owner reporting.

### JTBD Statement
> **When** I receive dozens of maintenance requests daily across multiple properties, **I want to** see an AI-triaged queue that automatically categorizes severity and assigns fault, **so I can** focus on truly urgent issues and reduce my coordination time by 40%.

## Persona 3: "刘师傅" (Liú Shīfu) — The Local Repair Master

### Demographics
| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 52 |
| Location | Sanya — operates within a 10 km radius |
| Tech Comfort | Low — uses WeChat voice messages and WeChat Pay, nothing else |
| Language | Mandarin + Hainan dialect |
| Specialization | Plumbing and general home repairs |
| Income | ¥6,000-12,000/month (variable, based on job volume) |

### Daily Workflow
- Gets jobs through word-of-mouth and WeChat group recommendations.
- Drives to potential clients' homes to give free estimates (costs him ¥50-100 per trip in fuel and time).
- Completes 2-4 jobs per day. Gets paid in cash or via WeChat Pay red envelopes.
- Has no online presence. No Meituan/Dianping listing.

### Core Frustrations
| Pain Point | Severity (1-5) |
|---|---|
| "Free estimate" trips waste time when the client doesn't hire him | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Unreliable job pipeline — feast or famine | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Clients don't describe the problem accurately — arrives with wrong tools | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Chasing landlords for payment after completing the job | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Cannot navigate complex apps or dashboards | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

### Goals
1. Receive pre-diagnosed, high-intent jobs pushed directly to WeChat.
2. Know exactly what tools and materials to bring before driving.
3. Get paid immediately upon job completion via escrow.

### JTBD Statement
> **When** I'm between repair jobs and available for work, **I want to** receive a WeChat notification with a pre-diagnosed job nearby that includes photos and material estimates, **so I can** accept it instantly, bring the right tools, and get paid immediately without chasing clients.
